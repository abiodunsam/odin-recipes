Fresh Garden Salad

Description

This fresh garden salad is crisp, colorful, and refreshing. It’s a simple mix of vegetables tossed with a light dressing, making it perfect as a side dish or a healthy lunch.

The ingredients are flexible, so you can easily customize the salad with your favorite veggies or toppings.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce or mixed greens
    • Fi
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Carrots
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place the greens in a large bowl and add the chopped vegetables.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice or vinegar.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss gently to combine and serve immediately.
