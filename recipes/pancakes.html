Fluffy Pancakes

Description

These fluffy pancakes are light, soft, and perfect for a cozy breakfast. They come together quickly with simple ingredients you probably already have at home.

Serve them warm with butter, maple syrup, or fresh fruit for a comforting and delicious start to your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake and cook until bubbles form.
  6. Flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
