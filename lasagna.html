Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese. It's a comforting dish perfect for family dinner or gatherings.

The recipe is simple to follow and results in a hearty, flavorful meal that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender.
  2. Cook the ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 180 degree celcius for 45 minutes.
  5. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
